# Crab Dash 🦀🐢🦎

A co-op game about a Turtle and an Axolotl dodging crabs to reach the safe zone.

## How to Play
1. Choose **1 Player** (Turtle) or **2 Players** (Turtle & Axolotl).
2. Dodge the crabs patrolling the middle of the ocean.
3. Reach the **Safe Zone** at the top of the screen.
4. In 2-player mode, **both** players must reach the safe zone to advance.
5. If you hit a crab, you get sent back to the starting line!
6. Complete **10 rounds** to win the game.

## Controls
- **Player 1 (Turtle 🐢):** WASD Keys
- **Player 2 (Axolotl 🦎):** Arrow Keys
