How to Play

Goal: Defend the wall for 5 minutes!

  • WASD / Arrows: Move character.
  • Left Click: Attack or place traps.
  • Bricks (Squares): Walk over them to pick up, then touch the Wall (bottom of screen) to repair it.
  • XP: Earned from kills. Spend it in the Shop.

Roles:

  • Builder: Places Fire Traps. Best at wall repair.
  • Defender: Melee Poison Blade. Slows monsters.
  • Mage: Ranged Fireballs. High AOE damage.
